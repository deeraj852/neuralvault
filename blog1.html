5 Tips for Using AI Tools as a Student

1. Use AI to Learn — Not to Cheat

The most successful students use AI as a learning partner, not a shortcut. Instead of asking AI to write your essay, ask it to explain the topic, give you an outline, and then write it yourself. This way you actually learn while still saving time.

2. Always Verify AI Information

AI tools can sometimes provide incorrect information, especially about very recent events or very specific facts. Always double check important facts from a reliable source before including them in your assignments. Perplexity AI is great for this because it always shows its sources.

3. Use Multiple Tools Together

The best student workflow combines multiple AI tools. Use Perplexity to research a topic, Claude to help structure and write your essay, and Canva AI to create a visual presentation. Each tool has its strength — use them together for the best results.

4. Check Your School's AI Policy

Many schools and universities have updated their policies on AI use. Some allow it freely, others require disclosure, and some prohibit it entirely for certain assignments. Always check your institution's guidelines before using AI for academic work.

5. Build AI Skills for Your Career

Learning to use AI tools effectively is now one of the most valuable skills you can have. Employers across every industry are looking for graduates who know how to work with AI. Start building these skills now and you will have a significant advantage when you enter the job market.
